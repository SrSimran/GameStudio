responsible play policy

We urge all users on Crickovers to play sensibly and responsibly. Note that our platform only allows players over 18 to play real-money skill games. To manage your gaming activities, we have a "Responsible Play Policy".

GUIDES FOR RESPONSIBLE PLAY

In certain instances, it can be hard for online gamers to know when their gaming is out of control. The potential harm from these issues is often minimized, hidden, or denied.

The best practices mentioned below can help you play responsibly:

Make a gaming budget.
Rather than wallowing in your losses, focus on the skill development the game offers.
Overindulgence should be avoided.
You shouldn't view playing games as an escape from reality.
Make sure you keep track of your expenses as well as the time you spend playing.
