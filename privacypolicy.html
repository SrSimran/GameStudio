In addition, Crickovers will not have access to any of your personal or sensitive information without your permission.

HOW YOUR INFORMATION IS USED

Your information is used primarily to deliver and optimize our offerings and improve our user-centricity. In order to provide you with a personalized user experience, process transactions, and respond to your inquiries and support requests, we utilize your personal information. Additionally, we may use non-personal information for analytical purposes in order to understand user trends and preferences in order to improve the performance and content of our website. We do not sell your personal information to third parties, and we maintain stringent security measures to protect your information.

Users' personal information will not be shared with third parties unless they give us permission to do so. PROTECTION OF INFORMATION

In order to stay ahead of emerging threats and to ensure that our users' information is protected, Crickovers continuously monitors and updates its security practices. Despite our efforts to protect user data, it is important that users understand that no method of transmission or storage can guarantee absolute security. You acknowledge and accept the inherent risks associated with the transmission and storage of data when you use our website or our services.

RETENTION OF INFORMATION

You may continue to access our services and participate in fantasy cricket competitions as long as you maintain an active account with us. We will retain your data for a limited period of time if you decide to delete your account in order to comply with any legal requirements or to resolve any disputes or inquiries you may have. Statistical analysis and research about users are conducted using anonymized and aggregated data, but no personally identifiable information is retained. As soon as the user data is no longer required, it is securely deleted or anonymized, ensuring that it cannot be associated with any specific individual. Nonetheless, users should be aware that certain data, such as transaction records or legal documentation, may be retained as required by law or for legitimate business reasons.
