fairplay policy

In order to maintain the integrity of the game, we are committed to promoting fair play. In our Fairplay policy, we ensure that all users adhere to a set of rules and guidelines that encourage fair competition, discourage cheating and unethical behavior, and ensure that all users are playing on an equal playing field.

EQUALITY

Crickovers is committed to treating all users fairly and equally, regardless of their gender, race, ethnicity, nationality, or any other aspect of their identity. Discrimination or bias based on any of these factors will be dealt with appropriately, including suspension or termination of the account if necessary. As we strive to foster an inclusive and diverse community, all fantasy cricket players have an equal opportunity to participate in fantasy cricket without facing prejudice or unfair treatment.

ADVANCED DEADLINES

Our goal is to provide timely and accurate information about upcoming matches and contests. It is not uncommon for us to set advanced deadlines for team selection or entry into contests in order to ensure that everything runs smoothly and fairly. If users fail to meet these deadlines, they may be excluded from participating in the contests they wish to participate in.

GAME RESULTS

The scores, player performances, and match results are updated after each game by our dedicated team. In addition to detailed match summaries, leaderboards, and individual player statistics, users can track their performance and compare it with that of other participants. In order to ensure a seamless and engaging experience, we strive to deliver reliable and comprehensive results.

USER TRUST

We place a high priority on gaining the trust of our users. As a result of implementing stringent policies and using advanced algorithms, we ensure that you do not cheat or gain an unfair advantage.

FAIR WINNINGS AND PAYOUTS

Our goal is to ensure fair payouts and winnings for all users. Our transparent and reliable system calculates points accurately and distributes winnings promptly. The payout process we offer is hassle-free, so users can enjoy their winnings without any delays or complications.

VIOLATION OF RULES AND ITS CONSEQUENCES

According to the law, we strictly prohibit users under 18 from participating in online or fantasy sports contests. In order to maintain fairness, Crickovers prohibits users from creating multiple accounts. The violation of this policy, including the creation of duplicate or fraudulent accounts, will result in immediate action, including permanent suspension and forfeiture of winnings associated with the account.
Impersonating other players, administrators, or anyone else is strictly forbidden. We take immediate action against any violation of our platform's fair play policy, including account suspensions and permanent bans. Crickovers does not offer services in Odisha, Arunachal pradesh, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Assam, Sikkim, and Nagaland because pay-to-play internet gaming is not legal in those states.
In the event that a user violates our Fair Play standards, they will have their account immediately disabled, and their wallet balance may be reduced to zero. It is also possible to bring legal charges against a user who violated our Fair Play standards.
